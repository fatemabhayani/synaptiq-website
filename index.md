## The SynaptIQ Cycle™: Learning Reimagined (8 AM – 3 PM)
Our students experience a full-day, purpose-driven model that integrates academic excellence, ethical grounding, and creative growth in four transformational stages.

| Phase | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Synapse Session™** | AI-personalized mastery in core subjects |
| **Deep Dive Lab™** | Problem-solving, Socratic thinking, and case-based application |
| **Morality & Ethics Circle™** | Guided exploration of values, ethical dilemmas, and life purpose |
| **Lunch & Movement Break** | Reflection, recharge, and social connection |
| **Human Experience Block™** | Emotional intelligence, civic engagement, and life design |
| **Creator’s Hour™** | Passion projects, liberal arts, digital making, entrepreneurship |

We don’t fill time — we design it intentionally. Students leave each day wiser, stronger, and more awake to the world.
