# Admissions & Enrollment

**Join a School That Values Who You Are — Not Just What You Score.**

## Begin Your SynaptIQ Journey
We don’t just admit students or professionals — we welcome curious minds, moral hearts, and purposeful learners. Whether you’re a parent looking for a better school experience for your child or a professional seeking growth in the age of AI, our admissions process is personalized, transparent, and supportive every step of the way.

## K–12 Admissions: Reimagining School for Your Child
### Who We're Looking For
- Students who are intellectually curious
- Families who value character, creativity, and connection
- Learners who want more than busywork or rote learning
- Children ready to engage in a school that blends AI personalization with moral mentorship

### K–12 Application Process
| Step | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **1. Inquiry Form** | Submit a quick interest form online so we can get to know you. |
| **2. Discovery Call** | A 30-minute virtual meeting with our team to understand your goals and answer questions. |
| **3. Student Profile** | Share recent academic records, interests, and learning needs. |
| **4. Family Interview** | Meet with the student and parents together — we want to hear your story, not just your grades. |
| **5. Learning Preview (Optional)** | Experience a trial day or virtual preview of the SynaptIQ environment. |
| **6. Decision & Enrollment** | Finalize your spot and prepare for a smooth onboarding. |

Applications are reviewed year-round with limited seats per cohort.

## Professional Stream Enrollment: Designed for the Busy Learner
### Who It’s For
- Working professionals, educators, leaders, and founders
- People seeking ethical clarity, real community, and adaptive skills
- Learners ready to engage in real-time, mentor-led evening sessions

### Enrollment Steps (Evening Programs)
| Step | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **1. Program Selection** | Choose your preferred track: AI Skills, Leadership, or Ethics. |
| **2. Application Form** | Share your background and goals so we can personalize the experience. |
| **3. Orientation Call** | Join a quick virtual meet-up to explore the structure and rhythm of the program. |
| **4. Confirmation** | Pay the program fee (or receive scholarship approval) and get your schedule + learning kit. |
| **5. Join the Cohort** | Become part of a live, engaged learning community that grows with you. |
