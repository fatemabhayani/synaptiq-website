Children should wake up excited to go to school — not exhausted by it. The K–12 Stream unlocks each student’s potential with a balance of:

## A Day in the Life of a SynaptIQ Learner (8 AM – 3 PM)
Every school day follows the SynaptIQ Cycle™, keeping students engaged, growing, and grounded.

| Time | Phase | Focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **8:00 – 9:30** | Synapse Session™ | Personalized academic learning in Math, Science, and Language using our adaptive AI engine |
| **9:30 – 11:00** | Deep Dive Lab™ | Socratic seminars, creative problem-solving, collaborative inquiry |
| **11:00 – 12:00** | Morality & Ethics Circle™ | Guided discussion on values, responsibility, and decision-making in an AI-driven world |
| **12:00 – 12:45** | Lunch & Play | Nutrition, mindfulness, social bonding, and open movement |
| **12:45 – 1:45** | Human Experience Block™ | Life skills: communication, empathy, leadership, and global citizenship |
| **1:45 – 3:00** | Creator’s Hour™ | Passion-based projects: design thinking, digital creation, entrepreneurship, art, science, culture |
